<p><p>The High Pressure Hydrogen Cylinder market features four main types based on construction materials and design. Type I cylinders are made of solid steel, offering durability but are heavy. Type II cylinders use a composite overwrapped design on a metal liner, reducing weight while retaining strength. Type III cylinders have a fully composite shell over a metal liner, further decreasing weight. Type IV cylinders are made entirely of composite materials, providing the lightest option with high resistance to corrosion, ideal for hydrogen storage.</p></p>
