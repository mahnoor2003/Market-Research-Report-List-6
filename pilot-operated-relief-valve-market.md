<p><p>Pilot-operated relief valves are critical safety devices used in various industries to manage pressure in systems. The market can be categorized into closed-type and non-closed-type valves. Closed-type pilot-operated relief valves maintain pressure in enclosed systems, preventing leaks and protecting against overpressure. In contrast, non-closed-type valves are suited for open systems where venting to the atmosphere is acceptable. Both types serve essential functions in preventing equipment damage and ensuring operational safety, catering to different application requirements.</p></p>
