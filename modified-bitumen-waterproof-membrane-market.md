<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen Waterproof Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS</li><li>APP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified bitumen waterproof membranes are categorized into two main types: Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS) and Atactic Polypropylene (APP). SBS membranes offer flexibility and elasticity, making them ideal for applications in colder climates, while APP membranes provide UV resistance and durability, suitable for warmer regions. Both types enhance the lifespan of roofing systems by providing robust waterproofing solutions, with SBS being favored for its performance in fluctuating temperatures, whereas APP is chosen for its resistance to aging and exposure to elements.</p></p>
