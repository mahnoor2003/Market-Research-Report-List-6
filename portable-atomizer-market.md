<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Atomizer</li><li>Ultrasonic Atomizer</li><li>Net Atomizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Atomizer market is divided into three main types: Pneumatic Atomizers, Ultrasonic Atomizers, and Net Atomizers. Pneumatic Atomizers use compressed air to create a fine mist, ideal for applications requiring a powerful spray. Ultrasonic Atomizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to produce ultra-fine droplets, suitable for delicate applications and efficient moisture delivery. Net Atomizers, also known as mesh atomizers, employ a vibrating mesh to create a consistent aerosol, providing precise control and reduced waste, making them popular in personal care and pharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
