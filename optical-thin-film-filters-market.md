<p><p>The optical thin-film filters market encompasses several types, including bandpass filters that allow a specific wavelength range while blocking others, and notch filters that selectively remove a narrow band of wavelengths. Shortpass edge filters transmit wavelengths shorter than a specified cutoff, while longpass edge filters allow longer wavelengths to pass. Dichroic filters reflect certain wavelengths while transmitting others, making them useful in applications like optical devices and imaging systems. Each filter type serves distinct roles in managing light for various technologies.</p></p>
