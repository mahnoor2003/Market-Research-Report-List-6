<p><p>Horse racing market types offer various betting options. A Win Bet is a wager on a horse to finish first. Each Way covers both winning and placing, offering returns if the horse finishes in predetermined positions. Single Bets focus on one selection, while Multiple Bets combine several selections, increasing potential returns but also risk. A Straight Forecast is a bet on two horses to finish first and second in a specific order. Reverse Forecast allows for either order, and a Tricast bets on the first three finishers in exact order.</p></p>
