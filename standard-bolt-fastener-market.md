<p><p>The standard bolt fastener market includes variations such as half screw bolts and full screw bolts. Half screw bolts feature a partially threaded shaft, allowing for secure fastening while enabling some movement, typically used in applications requiring adjustment. In contrast, full screw bolts have a fully threaded shaft, ensuring a stronger grip and suitable for heavy load applications. Both types are essential in construction and manufacturing, with distinct roles catering to different structural and mechanical needs.</p></p>
