<p><p>The Linear Variable Displacement Transducer (LVDT) market comprises two primary types: AC LVDTs and DC LVDTs. AC LVDTs utilize alternating current to measure displacement, offering high precision and robustness in dynamic applications. In contrast, DC LVDTs operate with direct current, providing simpler signal processing and better integration with digital systems. Both types are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where accurate position sensing and measurement are critical for control and automation.</p></p>
