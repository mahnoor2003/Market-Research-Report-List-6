<p><p>The X-ray foreign detector market comprises two primary types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). Digital Radiography utilizes digital sensors to capture images instantly, enhancing diagnostic speed and efficiency. It is widely used in various medical applications due to its ability to provide real-time imaging with lower radiation doses. In contrast, Computed Tomography combines multiple X-ray images to create detailed cross-sectional views of internal structures, aiding in more comprehensive diagnoses while offering advanced visualization capabilities for various clinical scenarios.</p></p>
