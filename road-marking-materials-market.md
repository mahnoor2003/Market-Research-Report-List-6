<p><p>The road marking materials market is primarily divided into two types: performance-based markings and paint-based markings. Performance-based markings encompass advanced materials designed to enhance visibility, durability, and safety, often incorporating reflective elements for better nighttime visibility. In contrast, paint-based markings are traditional solutions that utilize various types of paint formulations to delineate road features. While paint-based options are generally more cost-effective and easier to apply, performance-based materials offer superior longevity and performance, making them ideal for high-traffic areas.</p></p>
