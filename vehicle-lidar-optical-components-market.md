<p><ul><li>Receiving End</li><li>Transmitting End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Lidar Optical Components Market is divided into two main types: the receiving end and the transmitting end. The receiving end includes components that capture reflected light signals, enabling the system to analyze the environment. This often involves photodetectors and lenses. The transmitting end consists of elements that emit laser pulses, such as laser diodes and optics that focus the light. Both components are crucial for ensuring accurate distance measurement and detailed 3D mapping for autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
