<p><p>The Open Loop Hall Effect Current Sensor market is divided into two primary types: Linear Output and Threshold Output. Linear Output sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the current flowing through the conductor, enabling precise measurements in various applications. In contrast, Threshold Output sensors activate at predefined current levels, ideal for overcurrent protection and monitoring scenarios. Both types cater to different requirements in industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics sectors, enhancing efficiency and safety in electrical systems.</p></p>
