<p><p>The cervical interbody devices market comprises three primary types: pre-filled, corpectomy, and expandable devices. Pre-filled devices come with bone graft material, promoting faster fusion. Corpectomy devices are used to replace removed vertebral bodies due to injury or disease, providing structural support. Expandable devices can be adjusted during surgery to fit the patient's anatomy, allowing for better alignment and stability. Each type addresses specific spinal conditions, enhancing recovery and reducing complications in cervical fusion procedures.</p></p>
