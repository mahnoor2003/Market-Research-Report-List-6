<p><strong>The Non contact Infrared Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li><li>SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-contact infrared temperature sensor market primarily comprises two types: through-hole thermopile infrared sensors and SMD (Surface Mount Device) thermopile infrared sensors. Through-hole sensors are designed for insertion into PCBs, offering robust performance in varied environments, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, SMD sensors are compact and suited for high-density electronic assemblies, providing efficient thermal management in consumer electronics and medical devices. Both types leverage thermopile technology for accurate temperature measurements without physical contact, enhancing their versatility across industries.</p></p>
