<p><p>The respirator medical batteries market encompasses various battery types essential for powering respiratory devices. Nickel Cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries are recognized for their durability and ability to perform in extreme temperatures but suffer from memory effect. Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries provide a greater capacity than Ni-Cd with less environmental impact. Alkaline-Manganese batteries are typically used in disposable applications, offering a cost-effective solution for low-drain devices.</p></p>
