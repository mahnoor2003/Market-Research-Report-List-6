<p><p>IoT Telecom Services market can be divided into four main types based on the technology used for communication: Cellular, LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network), NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT), and RF-Based. Cellular networks provide reliable and high-speed communication, LPWANs are designed for low power consumption and long-range connectivity, NB-IoT is ideal for low-cost, low-power devices, and RF-Based technology uses radio frequency for wireless communication. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
