<p><p>The refrigerant market includes various types: HCFCs (hydrochlorofluorocarbons) are partially phased out due to their ozone-depleting potential but still used in some applications. HFCs (hydrofluorocarbons) are a widely used alternative, although they are being regulated for their high global warming potential. HCs (hydrocarbons) are natural refrigerants known for low environmental impact and high efficiency. Other refrigerants include newer alternatives like HFOs (hydrofluoroolefins), designed to reduce both ozone depletion and global warming impacts, improving sustainability in cooling technologies.</p></p>
