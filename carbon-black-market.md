<p><strong>The Carbon Black Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Black</li><li>Furnace Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon black market comprises several types, primarily thermal black and furnace black. Thermal black is produced using a gas furnace process, resulting in low structure and high purity, making it suitable for applications like pigments and coatings. Furnace black, on the other hand, is generated through the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, yielding a higher structure and greater reinforcement properties, ideal for tires and rubber products. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, plastics, and coatings.</p></p>
