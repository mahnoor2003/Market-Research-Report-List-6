<p><p>Tailings Storage Facilities (TSFs) are critical structures for managing waste generated from mineral processing. Market types include dry stacking, where dewatered tailings are compacted and stored on land, minimizing water usage and environmental risk. Underground storage involves depositing tailings into mined-out areas, reducing surface footprint and potential contamination. Other methods may include traditional wet storage in dams or lagoons. Each type balances economic, environmental, and safety considerations while addressing the unique challenges of tailings management.</p></p>
