<p><p>The Interventional X-ray Contrast and Imaging Agents market comprises several types. Iodinated contrast media, primarily used in CT scans and angiography, enhance vascular visualization. Barium-based contrast media are essential for gastrointestinal imaging, providing clear delineation of the digestive tract. Microbubble contrast media are utilized in ultrasound imaging, improving the visualization of blood flow and tissue perfusion. Additionally, other contrast agents include gadolinium for MRI, which is used to enhance tissue contrast, expanding diagnostic capabilities across various imaging modalities.</p></p>
