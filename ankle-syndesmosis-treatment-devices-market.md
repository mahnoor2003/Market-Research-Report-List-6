<p><p>The ankle syndesmosis treatment devices market includes two primary fixation types: suture button fixation and screw fixation. Suture button fixation involves using a button-like device and sutures to stabilize the syndesmosis ligament, providing flexibility and low-profile support. Screw fixation uses a self-tapping screw to secure the syndesmosis and offers robust stability but may limit dorsiflexion. Both methods aim to facilitate healing and restore ankle stability, catering to varying patient needs and anatomical considerations in treatment.</p></p>
