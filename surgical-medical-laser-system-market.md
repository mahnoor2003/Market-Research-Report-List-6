<p><p>The surgical medical laser system market encompasses various types of laser technologies. Solid-state laser systems use solid crystals to produce coherent light, known for their reliability and efficiency in surgeries. Gas laser systems utilize gases as the lasing medium, offering high precision, particularly in delicate procedures. Dye laser systems employ organic dye as the medium, allowing for a range of wavelengths and applications in soft tissue surgery. Diode laser systems are compact, versatile, and widely used for their adaptability in various surgical environments.</p></p>
