<p><ul><li>Casting Molding Solid Surface</li><li>Extrusion Molding Solid Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid surface material market comprises two main types: casting molding solid surface and extrusion molding solid surface. Casting molding involves pouring a liquid resin mix into molds to create intricate designs and shapes, resulting in a seamless, non-porous surface ideal for countertops and sinks. In contrast, extrusion molding involves pushing heated materials through a die to produce continuous sheets of solid surface, allowing for larger and more uniform products. Both methods offer durability and aesthetic versatility in various applications.</p></p>
