<p><ul><li>Electromechanical Type Induction Meter</li><li>Electronic Energy Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase meter market consists primarily of two types: electromechanical induction meters and electronic energy meters. Electromechanical induction meters utilize a moving coil mechanism driven by magnetic fields to measure energy consumption, offering durability and long-lasting performance. Conversely, electronic energy meters employ digital technology to provide accurate readings, enhanced features like remote monitoring, and improved data analytics capabilities. Both types serve residential and commercial sectors, though electronic meters are increasingly favored for their smart functionalities and precision.</p></p>
