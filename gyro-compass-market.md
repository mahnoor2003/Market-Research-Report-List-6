<p><strong>The Gyro Compass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Optic Gyro Compass</li><li>Mechanical Gyro Compass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gyro compass market is primarily divided into two main types: fiber optic gyro (FOG) and mechanical gyro compasses. Fiber optic gyro compasses utilize the interference of light signals, providing high accuracy, reliability, and minimal drift, making them ideal for modern navigation systems. In contrast, mechanical gyro compasses operate based on the principles of angular momentum and are more traditional, typically found in older systems. Each type serves distinct applications depending on the requirements for precision, size, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
