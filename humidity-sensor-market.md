<p><strong>The Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humidity sensor market is primarily divided into two types: relative humidity sensors and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the moisture level in the air compared to the maximum amount that air can hold at a given temperature, providing a percentage value. In contrast, absolute humidity sensors quantify the actual amount of water vapor present in the air regardless of temperature, expressed in grams per cubic meter. Both types are essential for various applications, including HVAC systems, agriculture, and industrial processes.</p></p>
