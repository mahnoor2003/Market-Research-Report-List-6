<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Laser</li><li>Acoustic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-sniper Detection System market comprises three main types: infrared, laser, and acoustic systems. Infrared systems utilize heat signatures to detect sniper activity by identifying body heat or weapon warmth. Laser systems employ laser beams that can pinpoint the origin of a shot through light reflection. Acoustic systems analyze sound waves produced by gunfire, determining the location of a sniper based on the time it takes for sound to reach multiple sensors. Each type offers unique advantages for enhanced security and response capabilities.</p></p>
