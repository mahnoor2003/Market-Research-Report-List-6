<p><p>MEMS probe cards are essential tools in semiconductor testing, and they can be categorized into vertical probe cards and cantilever probe cards. Vertical probe cards use a vertical alignment of probes for efficient contact with the semiconductor device, offering high density and precision for advanced applications. Cantilever probe cards, on the other hand, employ a cantilever design that allows for flexibility and adaptability during testing, making them suitable for various package types. Both types cater to the growing demand for miniaturized and high-performance electronic devices.</p></p>
