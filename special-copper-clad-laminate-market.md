<p><p>The Special Copper Clad Laminate market includes various types of materials tailored for specific applications. BT/epoxy fiberglass cloth boards offer high thermal and electrical insulation. Modified FR-4 features low Coefficient of Thermal Expansion (CTE) and low dielectric constant/dissipation factor, enhancing performance. PPO modified epoxy boards improve thermal stability. BT-like boards provide similar properties to BT/epoxy. PTFE boards are known for their excellent chemical resistance. Hydrocarbon boards offer lightweight alternatives, while PI/glass fiber cloth boards excel in high-temperature resistance. Other markets include specialized composites for niche applications.</p></p>
