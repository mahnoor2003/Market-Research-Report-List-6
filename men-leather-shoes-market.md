<p><p>The men's leather shoes market offers various types of leather, each with distinct characteristics. Patent leather is glossy and often used for formal shoes, providing a sophisticated look. Pebble leather features a textured surface, making it durable and resistant to wear. Full-grain leather is the highest quality, retaining the natural grain for strength and breathability. Top-grain leather, slightly processed, is softer and more uniform. Suede leather, made from the underside of the hide, has a velvety finish, ideal for casual styles.</p></p>
