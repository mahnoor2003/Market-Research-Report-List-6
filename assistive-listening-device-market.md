<p><p>Assistive Listening Device market includes various types such as Infrared Hearing Systems, Hearing Loop Systems, Amplified Telephones Systems, Personal Hearing Amplifiers, and Others. Infrared Hearing Systems use infrared technology to transmit audio signals directly to a receiver worn by the user. Hearing Loop Systems use electromagnetic fields to send sound to hearing aids with a telecoil feature. Amplified Telephones Systems provide enhanced volume and clarity for phone conversations. Personal Hearing Amplifiers are small devices that amplify sounds for individuals with hearing loss. Other types include FM systems and Bluetooth hearing devices.</p></p>
