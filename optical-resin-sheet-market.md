<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Material</li><li>Thermoplastic Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical resin sheet market is primarily divided into two types: thermosetting and thermoplastic materials. Thermosetting materials are rigid and durable, providing excellent heat resistance and stability, making them ideal for applications that require permanent shape retention. In contrast, thermoplastic materials are flexible and can be remelted and reshaped, allowing for easier processing and recycling. Both types serve distinct roles in various industries, such as electronics, automotive, and consumer goods, catering to diverse performance and design needs.</p></p>
