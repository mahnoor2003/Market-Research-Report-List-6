<p><strong>The Dust Collection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Collection Systems</li><li>Electric Collection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dust collection systems are essential for maintaining air quality in various industrial settings. Mechanical collection systems utilize physical barriers like filters, cyclones, and precipitators to capture dust particles through airflow. These systems are energy-efficient and low-maintenance. On the other hand, electric collection systems employ electrostatic forces to attract and capture dust. While they generally offer higher efficiency for fine particles, they can be more complex and costly to implement. Both systems play crucial roles in enhancing workplace safety and compliance.</p></p>
