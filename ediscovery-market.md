<p><p>eDiscovery market consists of various types of services, such as Early Case Assessment (ECA), Processing, Review, Forensic Data Collection, Legal Hold Management, and Others. Forensic Data Collection is the gathering of digital evidence. Legal Hold Management ensures data is preserved for legal proceedings. Other services in the market cater to specific needs and requirements of clients in the eDiscovery process.</p></p>
