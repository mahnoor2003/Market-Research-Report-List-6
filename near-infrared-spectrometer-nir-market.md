<p><p>The Near Infrared Spectrometer (NIR) market comprises various types including Fourier Transform Near Infrared (FT-NIR), UV-Vis-NIR, Raman, and others. FT-NIR utilizes Fourier transform technology for high-resolution spectroscopy, while UV-Vis-NIR combines ultraviolet and visible light with near-infrared for comprehensive analysis. Raman spectroscopy relies on inelastic scattering of photons to provide molecular composition insights. Other types may include portable and handheld devices, offering versatility for applications in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
