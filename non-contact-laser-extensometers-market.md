<p><p>The Non-Contact Laser Extensometers market includes two primary types: Built-in Laser Extensometers and Frame Laser Extensometers. Built-in Laser Extensometers are integrated directly into test setups, offering a compact solution for measuring strain and displacement with high precision in various applications. Frame Laser Extensometers, on the other hand, are standalone devices mounted on test specimens, providing flexibility and ease of use across different testing scenarios. Both types leverage laser technology to ensure accurate, non-invasive measurements, making them valuable in materials testing and structural analysis.</p></p>
