<p><strong>The Fishmeal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam dried(SD)</li><li>Flame dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fishmeal market is primarily categorized into steam-dried (SD) and flame-dried (FD) types, each with distinct production methods and applications. Steam-dried fishmeal is produced using steam under pressure, resulting in a high-protein, low-ash product ideal for aquaculture and livestock feeds. In contrast, flame-dried fishmeal employs high-temperature direct flame, creating a product that may have different nutrient profiles and flavors. Both types serve vital roles in the animal feed industry, catering to specific nutritional needs and cost considerations.</p></p>
