<p><p>Modified ABS plastics are differentiated primarily into two market types: flame retardant and impact-resistant. Flame retardant ABS is designed to inhibit combustion, making it suitable for applications in electronics, automotive, and construction where fire safety is crucial. On the other hand, impact-resistant ABS offers enhanced toughness and durability, ideal for products requiring higher resilience against physical stress, such as automotive parts and consumer goods. Both types cater to specific industry needs, balancing safety and performance in various applications.</p></p>
