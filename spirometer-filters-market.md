On the other hand, filters for air market are used to eliminate airborne particles and bacteria from the inhaled and exhaled air, maintaining a clean and hygienic environment during spirometry tests. Both types of filters play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and reliability of spirometry measurements.</p></p>
