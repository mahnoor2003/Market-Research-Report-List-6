Iminodiacetate type resins effectively bind metal ions, making them suitable for water treatment and metal recovery. Polyamine type resins utilize amine groups to form stable complexes with metals, often used in pharmaceuticals. Glucamine type resins feature sugar-derived structures, enhancing selectivity for specific metal ions.
