<p><ul><li>Axial Plastic Film Capacitors</li><li>Radial Plastic Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic Film Capacitors are classified into two main types based on their design: Axial Plastic Film Capacitors and Radial Plastic Film Capacitors. Axial Plastic Film Capacitors have lead wires on both ends and are typically used in applications where space is limited. Radial Plastic Film Capacitors have lead wires on one end and are commonly used in electronic circuits. Both types of capacitors are widely used in various industries for filtering, coupling, and timing applications due to their high insulation resistance and low dielectric absorption.</p></p>
