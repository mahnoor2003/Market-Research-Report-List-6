<p><p>The medical ultrasound probe market includes different types such as linear type, convex type, phased array type, endocavitary type, and others. Linear type probes are used for superficial imaging, while convex type probes are used for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array probes are used for cardiac and vascular imaging. Endocavitary probes are used for gynecological and urological imaging. Other types of probes cater to specific applications such as transesophageal echocardiography or intracavity imaging. Each type of probe serves a unique purpose in medical imaging.</p></p>
