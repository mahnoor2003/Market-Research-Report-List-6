<p><p>Non-Melanoma Skin Cancer (NMSC) treatments encompass various modalities, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and photodynamic therapy. Chemotherapy for NMSC typically involves topical agents to target local tumors, especially for basal cell carcinoma and squamous cell carcinoma. Radiation therapy utilizes high-energy rays to destroy cancer cells, often for patients unable to undergo surgery. Photodynamic therapy combines light-sensitive medication with light exposure to selectively target cancer cells, providing a minimally invasive option. These treatments cater to different patient needs based on cancer type and stage.</p></p>
