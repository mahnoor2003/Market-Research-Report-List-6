<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The External Fixator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cycle</li><li>Unilateral</li><li>Mixing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external fixator market is categorized into three primary types: cycle, unilateral, and mixing. Cycle fixators are designed for temporary stabilization during complex treatments and can be adjusted throughout the healing process. Unilateral fixators, on the other hand, provide support from one side of the bone, ideal for simpler fractures. Mixing markets refer to products that combine features of both cycle and unilateral fixators, offering versatility for various orthopedic applications, enabling tailored treatment solutions for patients.</p></p>
