<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Shipborne Radars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) Radar</li><li>Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) Radar</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shipborne radars market is categorized primarily into three types: Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) Radar, Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) Radar, and others. PESA radars utilize a single transmitter to energize multiple antenna elements, offering robust performance in target detection and tracking. AESA radars, on the other hand, feature individually controlled antennas, enhancing angular resolution and frequency agility, making them superior for modern naval operations. The "others" category includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems, catering to various naval requirements.</p></p>
