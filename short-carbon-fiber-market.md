<p><strong>The Short Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch – Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Rayon – Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The short carbon fiber market is primarily segmented into three types: PAN-based carbon fibers, pitch-based carbon fibers, and rayon-based carbon fibers. PAN-based fibers, derived from polyacrylonitrile, are known for their high tensile strength and thermal stability, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Pitch-based fibers, produced from petroleum or coal tar, offer superior electrical conductivity and heat resistance, suitable for specialized applications. Rayon-based fibers, derived from cellulose, provide lower cost and moderate performance, often used in textiles and insulation.</p></p>
