<p><strong>The Copper Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Sintered</li><li>Medium Temperature Sintered</li><li>High Temperature Sintered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper paste market is categorized into three types based on sintering temperatures. Low temperature sintered copper paste enables bonding at temperatures below 300°C, making it ideal for heat-sensitive substrates. Medium temperature sintered variants operate between 300°C and 600°C, offering a balance between thermal and mechanical properties suitable for various electronics applications. High temperature sintered copper paste requires temperatures above 600°C, providing superior thermal conductivity and durability, often used in demanding environments like power electronics and automotive applications.</p></p>
