<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Turbochargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial flow</li><li>Axial flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust Gas Turbochargers (EGTs) are primarily categorized into radial flow and axial flow types based on their design and airflow characteristics. Radial flow turbochargers, which are more commonly used in automotive applications, utilize a centrifugal force to compress exhaust gases, enhancing power and efficiency. In contrast, axial flow turbochargers facilitate a more linear flow of gases, making them suitable for larger engines and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in improving engine performance and reducing emissions.</p></p>
