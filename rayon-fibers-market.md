<p><strong>The Rayon Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscose Staple Fiber</li><li>Viscose Filament Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rayon fibers are categorized mainly into two types: viscose staple fiber and viscose filament fiber. Viscose staple fiber is short, used primarily in textiles and apparel, providing a soft, absorbent, and breathable fabric ideal for everyday clothing. In contrast, viscose filament fiber consists of continuous strands, offering a lustrous finish and smooth texture, making it suitable for high-end fabrics and home furnishings. Both types are valued for their versatility, eco-friendliness, and comfort properties in the textile industry.</p></p>
