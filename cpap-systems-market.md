<p><strong>The CPAP Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pressure CPAP Device</li><li>Auto Adjusting CPAP Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPAP systems market features two primary types: Fixed Pressure CPAP devices and Auto Adjusting CPAP devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP devices deliver a constant airway pressure, providing consistent support for patients with obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, Auto Adjusting CPAP devices, also known as AutoPAP, dynamically adjust the pressure based on real-time breathing patterns, offering personalized treatment. This flexibility enhances comfort and effectiveness, making these systems increasingly popular among users seeking tailored sleep apnea management solutions.</p></p>
