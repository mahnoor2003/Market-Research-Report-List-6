<p><p>The virus testing diagnostic kits market includes various types of testing methods. Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) Test Kits use fluorescent-labeled antibodies to detect viral infections quickly. Lateral Flow Assay Test Kits provide rapid results through a simple strip format. Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR)-Based Test Kits amplify viral RNA for precise detection. Agglutination Assay Test Kits utilize clumping reactions for identifying viruses, while Flow-Through and Solid-Phase Assay Test Kits leverage liquid or solid substrates for effective viral identification.</p></p>
