<p><p>The semiconductor-based temperature sensor market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Voltage Output Temperature Sensors** provide a voltage signal proportional to temperature changes. </p><p>2. **Current Output Temperature Sensors** deliver a current signal that varies with temperature, ensuring high accuracy. </p><p>3. **Digital Output Temperature Sensors** offer direct digital readings, simplifying integration with digital systems. </p><p>4. **Resistance Output Silicon Temperature Sensors** leverage resistance changes in silicon to detect temperature variations. </p><p>5. **Diode Temperature Sensors** rely on forward voltage drop characteristics of diodes, offering high precision in temperature measurement.</p></p>
