ICT functional test probes focus on in-circuit testing to ensure chips function correctly while minimizing damage. BGA test probes specifically target ball grid array packages, allowing for effective testing of connections beneath the chip. These testing solutions are essential for quality assurance in semiconductor manufacturing and electronic device performance.</p></p>
