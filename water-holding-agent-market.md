<p><p>A water holding agent, also known as a moisture-retaining polymer, is a substance that enhances soil's ability to retain moisture, thereby improving water efficiency in agricultural practices. Water holding agents work by absorbing water and releasing it slowly to plants, promoting healthy growth and reducing the need for frequent irrigation.</p><p>The Water Holding Agent Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period.
