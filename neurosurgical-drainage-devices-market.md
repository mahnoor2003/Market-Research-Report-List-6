<p><ul><li>Shunt Types</li><li>Anti-Siphon Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurosurgical drainage devices market comprises various types aimed at managing cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) abnormalities. Shunt types, including ventriculoperitoneal shunts and ventriculoatrial shunts, facilitate fluid drainage to prevent intracranial pressure. Anti-siphon devices are specialized mechanisms integrated into shunts to prevent excessive drainage caused by gravitational siphoning. Together, these devices play a critical role in treating conditions like hydrocephalus and ensuring optimal cerebrospinal fluid balance, enhancing patient outcomes and reducing complications associated with cerebrospinal fluid management.</p></p>
