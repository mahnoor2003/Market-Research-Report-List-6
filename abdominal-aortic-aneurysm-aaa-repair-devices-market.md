<p><strong>The Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm (AAA) Repair Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synthetic Graft System</li><li>Endovascular Stent Graft System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm (AAA) Repair Devices market primarily includes two types: synthetic graft systems and endovascular stent graft systems. Synthetic graft systems involve traditional open surgery using a stitched graft to replace the aneurysm section. In contrast, endovascular stent graft systems utilize a minimally invasive approach, where a stent graft is inserted through the blood vessels, reducing recovery time and complication rates. Both systems aim to reinforce vascular integrity and prevent aneurysm rupture, catering to different patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
