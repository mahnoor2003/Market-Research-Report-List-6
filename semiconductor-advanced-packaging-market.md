<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Advanced Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging(FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging(FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip(FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor advanced packaging market includes several key types: Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP) offers greater die density and improved thermal performance by spreading connections over a larger area. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP) utilizes a more compact design with bonds on the chip’s surface, ideal for small devices. Flip Chip (FC) technology connects the chip directly to the substrate using solder bumps, enhancing electrical performance. 2.5D/3D packaging integrates multiple chips in a single package, improving functionality and efficiency through vertical stacking.</p></p>
