<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi routers are categorized into three types based on their frequency bands: dual-band, tri-band, and quad-band. Dual-band systems operate on 2.4GHz and 5GHz frequencies, making them suitable for basic home networking. Tri-band routers add an additional 5GHz band, enhancing performance for multiple devices and reducing congestion. Quad-band systems incorporate even more frequencies, often including a second 5GHz and a 6GHz band, providing maximum speed and capacity for high-demand environments, such as smart homes or businesses with numerous connected devices.</p></p>
