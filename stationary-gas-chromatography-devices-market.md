<p><strong>The Stationary Gas Chromatography Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary gas chromatography devices are classified into two main types: gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC utilizes a solid stationary phase to separate gas mixtures based on adsorption processes, making it effective for separating permanent gases and volatiles. In contrast, GLC employs a liquid stationary phase coated on a solid support, allowing for the separation of various organic compounds in gaseous samples. Both techniques are essential for diverse applications in chemical analysis, environmental monitoring, and quality control.</p></p>
