<p><ul><li>SEA Type</li><li>SEB Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silver bonding wires market is primarily categorized into SEA Type and SEB Type. SEA Type wires, or "Softly Entered Alloy," are designed for high-performance applications, offering excellent electrical conductivity and thermal resistance, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor packaging. In contrast, SEB Type wires, or "Softly Entered Bond," are engineered to provide superior reliability in environments with mechanical stress. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the electronics industry, supporting different manufacturing processes and product specifications.</p></p>
