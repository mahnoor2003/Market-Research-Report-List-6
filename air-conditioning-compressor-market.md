<p><ul><li>Piston Compressor</li><li>Rotary Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning compressor market primarily features two types: piston compressors and rotary compressors. Piston compressors utilize a reciprocating motion to compress refrigerant gas, offering high efficiency and performance for larger applications. In contrast, rotary compressors use a rotating mechanism, providing a compact design and quieter operation, making them ideal for residential and light commercial applications. Both types play crucial roles in HVAC systems, catering to diverse consumer needs based on efficiency, size, and noise levels.</p></p>
