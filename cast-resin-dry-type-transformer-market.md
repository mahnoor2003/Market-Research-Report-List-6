<p><strong>The Cast Resin Dry-Type Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concentric Type</li><li>Overlap Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast resin dry-type transformer market is segmented mainly into concentric and overlap types. Concentric type transformers feature coils arranged in concentric circles, optimizing space and cooling, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, overlap type transformers have overlapping windings that enhance magnetic coupling, allowing for compact designs with improved efficiency. Both types are increasingly preferred for their safety, low maintenance, and environmental benefits, especially in urban and industrial settings where fire hazards are a concern.</p></p>
