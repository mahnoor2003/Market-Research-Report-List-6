<p><ul><li>On Board</li><li>On Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flight Data Monitoring System market consists of two main types: On Board and On Ground. On Board systems are installed on aircraft to collect real-time data during flight, such as speed, altitude, and engine performance. This data is then used for monitoring and analysis to improve safety and efficiency. On Ground systems, on the other hand, collect data from aircraft once they have landed and are used for post-flight analysis and maintenance purposes. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing overall flight operations and safety.</p></p>
