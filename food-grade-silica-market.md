<p><p>The food grade silica market primarily consists of two types: precipitated silica and fumed silica. Precipitated silica is produced through a chemical process and is widely used as an anti-caking agent in food products, enhancing flowability and texture. Fumed silica, created by high-temperature processes, offers high surface area and is used for thickening and stabilizing formulations. Both types are critical in ensuring product quality, extending shelf life, and improving the characteristics of various food applications.</p></p>
