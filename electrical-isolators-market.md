<p><strong>The Electrical Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical isolators market comprises three main types: single break isolators, double break isolators, and pantograph type isolators. Single break isolators feature a single contact point for effective disconnection, while double break isolators have two contact points, enhancing reliability and performance. Pantograph type isolators are typically used in overhead transmission lines and are designed for high voltage applications. Each type serves specific operational needs in electrical systems, contributing to safety and maintenance in power distribution.</p></p>
