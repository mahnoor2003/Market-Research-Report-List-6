<p><p>Industrial calorimetric flow meters are essential instruments used to measure the flow rates of fluids based on thermal principles. The market can be segmented into two main types: for liquids and for gases. Calorimetric flow meters for liquids typically utilize thermal dispersion or resistance methods, providing precise measurements for various viscosities. For gases, these meters generally employ thermal mass flow techniques, effectively capturing low flow rates and ensuring accuracy across different gas compositions, making them critical for various industrial applications.</p></p>
