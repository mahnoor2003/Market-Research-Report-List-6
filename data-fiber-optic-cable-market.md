<p><p>The data fiber optic cable market is primarily categorized into two types: single mode and multimode cables. Single mode cables feature a small core diameter, allowing only one light mode to propagate, making them ideal for long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multimode cables have a larger core that supports multiple light modes, making them suitable for shorter distances within buildings or campuses. Each type serves distinct applications, optimizing performance based on specific network requirements.</p></p>
