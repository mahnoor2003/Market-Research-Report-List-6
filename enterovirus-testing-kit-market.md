<p><strong>The Enterovirus Testing Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RT-PCR</li><li>Rapid Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enterovirus Testing Kit market encompasses two primary types: RT-PCR and Rapid Test kits. RT-PCR (Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction) kits are highly sensitive and specific, allowing for the detection of enterovirus genetic material, thus enabling accurate diagnosis of infections. Rapid Test kits, on the other hand, provide quicker results, often within hours, by detecting viral antigens or antibodies. While RT-PCR is preferred for its accuracy, Rapid Tests are valued for their convenience and speed, particularly in resource-limited settings.</p></p>
