<p><p>CPR masks are essential medical devices used during cardiopulmonary resuscitation to provide a barrier between the rescuer and the patient, facilitating effective rescue breaths. These masks, often equipped with one-way valves, ensure that the rescuer can deliver air to the victim while minimizing the risk of contamination. As awareness about sudden cardiac arrest and the importance of immediate intervention grows, the demand for CPR masks is rising.</p><p>The CPR Masks Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.20% during the forecast period. Key driving factors include increasing instances of cardiovascular diseases, a rise in training programs for CPR, and growing emphasis on first aid readiness in public spaces. Technological advancements are leading to the development of more user-friendly, portable, and cost-effective CPR masks, catering to both professional healthcare providers and laypersons.</p><p>Recent trends show a surge in online training modules and digital awareness campaigns, which are further enhancing market growth. Additionally, the integration of CPR masks in automated external defibrillator (AED) kits is becoming more common, promoting their use in emergency situations. The focus on safety and hygiene is also influencing product designs, leading to improved materials and features that resonate with consumer preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>The CPR masks market includes various types designed for specific respiratory needs. Facial masks provide a secure seal over the mouth and nose for effective ventilation. Laryngeal masks are used to maintain an open airway during resuscitation, ensuring breath delivery into the trachea. Oral masks allow for direct mouth-to-mouth resuscitation with protective features, while nasal pillows, primarily used in CPAP therapy, fit snugly in the nostrils to provide continuous airflow. Each type serves distinct applications in emergency and therapeutic settings.</p></p>
