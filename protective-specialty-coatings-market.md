<p><strong>The Protective & Specialty Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Protective & Specialty Coatings market comprises three main types: solvent-borne coatings, which use organic solvents for application, offering high performance and durability but with environmental concerns; water-borne coatings, which utilize water as a solvent, providing lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and easier clean-up, making them more environmentally friendly; and powder coatings, which are applied as a dry powder and cured under heat, resulting in a tough finish and minimal waste. Each type serves distinct applications and industry needs.</p></p>
