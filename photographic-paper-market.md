<p><p>The photographic paper market consists of two main types: silver halide photographic paper and inkjet photographic paper. Silver halide paper is used primarily in traditional photography, relying on chemical processes to produce high-quality images with rich detail and contrast. In contrast, inkjet photographic paper is designed for digital printing, accommodating various inks to deliver vibrant colors and sharpness. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with silver halide favored by purists and inkjet favored for versatility in digital workflows.</p></p>
