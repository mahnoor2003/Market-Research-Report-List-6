<p><strong>The Temperature and Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Resistive Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature and humidity sensor market is primarily categorized into three types: capacitive, resistive, and others. Capacitive sensors measure humidity by detecting changes in electrical capacitance due to moisture in the air, offering high accuracy and stability. Resistive sensors, on the other hand, gauge humidity by measuring changes in electrical resistance as moisture levels fluctuate, making them cost-effective but less precise. Other types encompass a variety of sensors, including thermal conductivity and optical sensors, each designed for specific applications and environments.</p></p>
