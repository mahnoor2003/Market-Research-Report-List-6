<p><strong>The Automotive Over the Air (OTA) Updates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SOTA</li><li>FOTA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Over the Air (OTA) updates are classified into two primary market types: SOTA and FOTA. SOTA (Software Over the Air) focuses on delivering software updates for applications and infotainment systems, enhancing user experience and functionality. FOTA (Firmware Over the Air), on the other hand, involves updating the vehicle's firmware, which governs critical systems like engine control and safety features. Both types enable manufacturers to improve vehicle performance, fix bugs, and introduce new features without requiring physical visits to dealerships.</p></p>
