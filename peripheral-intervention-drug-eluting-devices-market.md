<p><strong>The Peripheral Intervention Drug-Eluting Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drug-Eluting Balloons</li><li>Drug-Eluting Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Intervention Drug-Eluting Devices market comprises two main types: Drug-Eluting Balloons (DEBs) and Drug-Eluting Stents (DESs). DEBs are intravascular balloons coated with therapeutic drugs that are released during inflation, helping to prevent restenosis in treated arteries. DESs, on the other hand, are stents coated with drugs that gradually elute, providing sustained drug release to inhibit cell proliferation and maintain vessel patency. Both devices aim to improve outcomes in peripheral vascular interventions by reducing complications and enhancing patient recovery.</p></p>
