<p><p>The pigment additives market is categorized into inorganic and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments, derived from mineral sources, are known for their durability, UV resistance, and non-toxic properties, making them suitable for applications in coatings, plastics, and construction. Organic pigments, on the other hand, are synthesized from carbon-based compounds and offer vibrant colors, excellent lightfastness, and versatility. They are widely used in textiles, inks, and paints, catering to industries that require bright hues and specific color performance.</p></p>
