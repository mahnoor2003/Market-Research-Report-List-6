<p><ul><li>Naso Gastric (NG) Feeding</li><li>Naso Duodenal Feeding</li><li>Naso Jejunal (NJ) Feeding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enteral Nutrition (EN) solutions encompass various feeding methods for patients unable to consume food orally. Naso Gastric (NG) feeding involves delivering nutrition directly to the stomach through a nasal tube, ideal for short-term use. Naso Duodenal feeding guides nutrients to the duodenum, suitable for patients with gastric issues. Naso Jejunal (NJ) feeding bypasses the stomach for direct delivery into the jejunum, often used for those with severe reflux. Other methods include surgical placements or specialized formulas tailored to specific needs or conditions.</p></p>
