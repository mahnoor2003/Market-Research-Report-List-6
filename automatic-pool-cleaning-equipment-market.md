<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaning Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaning equipment market features three primary types: suction-side cleaners, pressure-side cleaners, and electric cleaners. Suction-side cleaners use the pool's filtration system to create suction, removing debris as they navigate the pool. Pressure-side cleaners utilize a separate pressure source to propel water and capture dirt, often requiring a booster pump. Electric pool cleaners, powered by electricity, operate independently of the pool's filtration system and feature advanced navigation and cleaning capabilities, making them highly efficient and user-friendly.</p></p>
