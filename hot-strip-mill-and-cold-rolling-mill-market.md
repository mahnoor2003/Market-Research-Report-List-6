<p><p>The hot strip mill and cold rolling mill market includes various rolling mill types designed for different production needs. Two-high rolling mills consist of two rollers, often used for thick materials. Four-high rolling mills incorporate two smaller rollers between two larger ones, enhancing thickness reduction and surface finish. Six-high rolling mills feature additional rollers, allowing for even greater control over material properties and thickness. Each type serves specific applications in steel and metal processing, catering to diverse industrial demands.</p></p>
