<p><p>Rotary torque transducers are used to measure the amount of torque or rotational force applied to a system. There are two main types of rotary torque transducers in the market - the dynamic type and the static type. Dynamic torque transducers are designed to measure torque fluctuations in real-time, making them ideal for applications requiring high-speed and dynamic torque measurements. Static torque transducers, on the other hand, are used for applications where the torque is constant or slowly changing, providing accurate and stable measurements over a longer period of time.</p></p>
