<p><p>Automotive Active Grille Shutters (AGSs) are aerodynamic devices installed in vehicle grilles that regulate airflow and enhance energy efficiency. They can be categorized into two main types: horizontal AGS and vertical AGS. Horizontal AGS features louvered or shutter designs that open and close based on temperature and speed, optimizing cooling and reducing drag. Vertical AGS operates similarly but is oriented vertically, effectively channeling airflow while maintaining vehicle aesthetics and improving fuel efficiency. Both types contribute to enhanced vehicle performance and sustainability.</p></p>
