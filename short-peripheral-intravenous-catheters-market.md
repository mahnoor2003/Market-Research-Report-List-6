<p><ul><li>Ported Short PIVC</li><li>Non-Ported Short PIVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ported Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) are designed with a port or opening near the tip, allowing for additional medication administration without having to reinsert the catheter. These are commonly used in situations where frequent medication changes are needed. On the other hand, Non-Ported Short PIVC do not have an additional port and are used for single medication infusion. Both types of PIVC play a vital role in providing intravenous access for patients in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
