<p><p>The Pneumatic Acute Care Ventilator market consists of two main types: non-invasive and invasive ventilators. Non-invasive ventilators provide respiratory support without the need for intubation, using masks or nasal interfaces to deliver oxygen and assist breathing. In contrast, invasive ventilators are used when patients require intubation, directly connecting to the airway for more controlled and precise ventilation. Both types are essential for treating respiratory distress in acute care settings, catering to varying patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
