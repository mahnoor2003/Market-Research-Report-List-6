<p><p>The Lithium Niobate Crystals market is primarily categorized into two types: Optical Grade and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Grade. Optical Grade Lithium Niobate Crystals are primarily used in photonic applications, including lasers, modulators, and optical frequency converters, due to their excellent electro-optic properties. In contrast, SAW Grade Lithium Niobate Crystals are designed for applications involving surface acoustic waves, such as filters, resonators, and sensors, playing a crucial role in communication and signal processing technologies.</p></p>
