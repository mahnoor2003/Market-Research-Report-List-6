<p><ul><li>Geared</li><li>Non-geared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler hub motors market is primarily divided into geared and non-geared types. Geared hub motors utilize a gear system to increase torque, providing better acceleration and climbing ability, making them suitable for varied terrains. In contrast, non-geared hub motors, also known as direct-drive motors, offer simplicity and efficiency, with fewer moving parts, resulting in reduced maintenance. Both types cater to different rider preferences, with geared motors favored for performance and non-geared motors appreciated for reliability and ease of use.</p></p>
