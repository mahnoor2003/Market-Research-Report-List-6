<p><strong>The PCB Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Oven</li><li>Selective Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering system market comprises three main types: wave soldering ovens, reflow ovens, and selective soldering ovens. Wave soldering is suited for through-hole components, using a wave of molten solder to create connections. Reflow ovens cater to surface-mount technology, using controlled heat to melt solder paste and secure components. Selective soldering combines both techniques, targeting specific areas for soldering without affecting surrounding components, making it ideal for mixed-technology boards and complex assemblies.</p></p>
