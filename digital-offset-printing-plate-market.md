<p><ul><li>Thermal CTP Plate</li><li>UV-CTP Plates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital offset printing plate market comprises primarily two types: Thermal CTP Plates and UV-CTP Plates. Thermal CTP Plates utilize heat to create images, allowing for high-quality and precise prints, often favored for their efficiency and low environmental impact. In contrast, UV-CTP Plates are exposed to ultraviolet light, offering enhanced durability and fast drying times, making them suitable for various substrates. Both types cater to diverse printing needs, optimizing production processes in commercial printing and packaging industries.</p></p>
