<p><ul><li>Paste Type</li><li>Flaky Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TPEG market is segmented into two main types: Paste Type and Flaky Type. Paste Type TPEG is characterized by a viscous consistency, making it ideal for applications requiring high adhesive strength, such as construction and coatings. It provides better compatibility with various materials. On the other hand, Flaky Type TPEG features a solid, granulated form, offering ease of handling and storage. This type is commonly used in formulations where rapid dissolution and processing are essential, such as in the manufacturing of resins and adhesives.</p></p>
