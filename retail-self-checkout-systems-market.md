<p><p>Retail self-checkout systems are categorized into three main types. Stand-alone self-checkout systems are independent kiosks placed in retail environments, allowing customers to manage their transactions autonomously. Wall-mounted self-checkout systems are fixed to walls, saving floor space while providing a user-friendly interface for customers. Countertop self-checkout systems are compact, designed to fit on counters, facilitating quick and efficient transactions in smaller retail settings. Each type enhances customer experience and operational efficiency in varying retail contexts.</p></p>
