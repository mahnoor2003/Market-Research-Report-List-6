<p><ul><li>D-type Package Boilers</li><li>A-type Package Boilers</li><li>O-type Package Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil package boiler market comprises three main types: D-type, A-type, and O-type package boilers. D-type boilers feature a straight tube design, offering compactness and efficient steam generation, suitable for various industrial applications. A-type boilers are characterized by a more expansive design, ideal for higher capacity needs, with better access for maintenance. O-type boilers have a circular design, providing enhanced thermal efficiency and space-saving benefits, making them viable for specific applications where layout constraints are a concern.</p></p>
