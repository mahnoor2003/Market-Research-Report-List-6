<p><p>The prestressed concrete (PC) wire and strand market comprises three main types: uncoated PC strand, galvanized PC strand, and epoxy-coated PC strand. Uncoated PC strand is primarily used for applications requiring high tensile strength without additional protection. Galvanized PC strand features a protective zinc coating, enhancing corrosion resistance, making it suitable for exposed environments. Epoxy-coated PC strand offers superior durability with a layer of epoxy, ideal for harsh conditions, further extending the lifespan and performance of prestressed concrete structures.</p></p>
