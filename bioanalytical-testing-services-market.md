<p><p>Bioanalytical testing services encompass the analysis of biological samples to measure drug concentrations and assess their pharmacokinetics. The market is categorized into two main types: small molecules and large molecules. Small molecules typically include low-molecular-weight compounds that can be synthesized chemically, often used in traditional pharmaceuticals. Large molecules consist of biologics, such as proteins or monoclonal antibodies, which require complex biotechnological processes for their production. Each type demands distinct testing methodologies and regulatory considerations, influenced by their unique characteristics.</p></p>
