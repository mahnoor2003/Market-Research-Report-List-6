<p><ul><li>Die- Level Packaging and Assembly Equipment</li><li>Wafer-Level Packaging and Assembly Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor packaging and assembly equipment market encompasses technologies that protect and connect semiconductor devices. Die-level packaging involves individual chips being encapsulated and mounted onto substrates for enhanced performance and reliability. On the other hand, wafer-level packaging allows for packaging at the wafer stage, enabling higher integration and reduced costs through space-efficient design. Both types serve to facilitate the efficient transfer of electrical signals while improving thermal management, thus catering to the evolving demands of electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
