<p><p>The marine fuel cell system market primarily comprises Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC). PEMFCs are known for their efficiency and quick startup, making them suitable for marine applications requiring high power density. Conversely, SOFCs operate at higher temperatures and excel in fuel flexibility and efficiency over longer durations, ideal for larger vessels. Both technologies contribute to reducing emissions and enhancing energy efficiency in the maritime industry, driving innovation in sustainable marine propulsion.</p></p>
