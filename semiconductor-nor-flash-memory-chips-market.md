In contrast, Parallel NOR Flash utilizes multiple data lines, enabling faster data transfer rates and improved performance for applications requiring high-speed access. Both types serve different needs in the growing sectors of consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, each with unique advantages.</p></p>
