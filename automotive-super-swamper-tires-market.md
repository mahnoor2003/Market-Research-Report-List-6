<p><p>The Automotive Super Swamper Tires market features two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with layers of fabric cords that run diagonally, offering durability and flexibility for off-road conditions. They provide excellent traction on rugged terrains but tend to wear faster. In contrast, radial tires have cords that run perpendicular to the tread, allowing for better fuel efficiency and a smoother ride. This design enhances handling and longevity, making them suitable for diverse driving conditions, including off-road applications.</p></p>
