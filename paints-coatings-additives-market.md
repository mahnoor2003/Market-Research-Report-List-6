<p><p>The paints and coatings additives market includes various types that enhance performance and application. Biocides prevent microbial growth, ensuring product durability. Dispersants and wetting agents improve pigment dispersion and enhance surface wetting for a smoother finish. Stabilizers maintain formulation consistency and prevent sedimentation. Defoamers eliminate air bubbles during production and application, improving the aesthetic appeal. Deaerators assist in removing trapped air from coatings, ensuring a flawless application. Together, these additives significantly improve paint and coating performance.</p></p>
