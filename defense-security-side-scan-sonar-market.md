<p><p>The Defense & Security Side Scan Sonar market can be categorized into single-beam and multi-beam systems. Single-beam sonar emits one focused beam, ideal for detecting objects on the seabed, providing high-resolution images but limited coverage. In contrast, multi-beam sonar generates multiple parallel beams, allowing for broader area scanning and faster data collection. This makes multi-beam systems particularly advantageous for extensive surveys and maritime security operations, where detailed mapping and quick analysis of larger areas are crucial.</p></p>
