<p><p>The advanced semiconductor packaging market encompasses several types, including Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP), which enhances performance and reduces size by integrating more components on a single die. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP) offers a more traditional approach with limited I/O capabilities. Flip Chip (FC) technology allows for direct electrical connection between the chip and substrate, improving signal integrity. Additionally, 2.5D/3D packaging integrates multiple chips for higher performance, while "Others" includes various innovative methods evolving in the market.</p></p>
