<p><p>The high-end ice wines market encompasses two primary types: white ice wine and red ice wine. White ice wines, typically made from varietals like Riesling or Vidal, are known for their vibrant acidity balanced with rich sweetness, offering flavors of peach, honey, and citrus. In contrast, red ice wines are crafted from red grapes such as Cabernet Franc, providing deeper, bolder flavors like berry and spice.
