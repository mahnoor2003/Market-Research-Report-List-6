<p><p>The multiplex molecular diagnostic market includes various PCR techniques that enhance detection capabilities. Real-time PCR offers rapid and quantitative results through fluorescent detection. Nested PCR with melt curve analysis increases specificity and sensitivity by amplifying target DNA in two steps while analyzing melting temperatures for verification. PCR with low-density microarrays allows simultaneous detection of multiple targets on a chip, while PCR with liquid phase bead arrays uses beads for efficient target capture. PCR with electrochemical detection leverages electrical signals for rapid, sensitive identification of DNA.</p></p>
