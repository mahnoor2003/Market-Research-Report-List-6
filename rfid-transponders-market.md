<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID Transponders</li><li>Active RFID Transponders</li><li>Semi-Active RFID Transponders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID transponders are electronic devices that store and transmit data wirelessly. Passive RFID transponders do not have a power source and rely on the RFID reader's energy to transmit data. Active RFID transponders have their own power source and can transmit data over longer distances. Semi-active RFID transponders also have their own power source but rely on the RFID reader for communication. These different types cater to various use cases, such as asset tracking, inventory management, and access control.</p></p>
