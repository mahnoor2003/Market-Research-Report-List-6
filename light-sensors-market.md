<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Light Sensor</li><li>Non-Contact Light Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact light sensors require physical contact with the object or surface they are measuring light from, allowing for accurate and precise readings. Examples include photoconductive cells and photodiodes. On the other hand, non-contact light sensors do not require physical contact with the object or surface and can measure light from a distance. Examples include phototransistors and photomultiplier tubes. Both types of sensors are used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation for applications like brightness control, proximity sensing, and object detection.</p></p>
