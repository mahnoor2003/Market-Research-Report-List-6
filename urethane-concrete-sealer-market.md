<p><p>The Urethane Concrete Sealer market is primarily categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based sealers. Water-based urethane sealers are known for their low VOC content, easier application, and quicker drying times, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for indoor use. In contrast, solvent-based urethane sealers offer superior durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-traffic areas and outdoor applications. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance and environmental considerations in concrete protection.</p></p>
