<p><strong>The Release Liners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Release Linear Paper</li><li>Release Linear Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The release liners market is primarily divided into two types: release liner paper and release liner film. Release liner paper is typically made from paper substrates coated with silicone, offering a cost-effective solution for various adhesive applications. In contrast, release liner film, often constructed from materials like PET or polyethylene, provides enhanced durability and moisture resistance. Both types are essential in industries such as packaging, labels, and adhesives, facilitating easy separation and application of sticky materials while protecting their adhesive properties.</p></p>
