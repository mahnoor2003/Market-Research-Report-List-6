<p><p>Cathode blocks for aluminum production come in three main types: semi-graphitic, graphitic, and graphitized. Semi-graphitic blocks contain a mix of graphite and other materials, offering moderate electrical conductivity and thermal resistance. Graphitic blocks are composed primarily of graphite, providing excellent conductivity and stability at high temperatures. Graphitized blocks undergo a specific treatment to enhance their properties, resulting in superior performance and longer service life. Each type caters to different operational efficiencies and cost considerations in aluminum smelting processes.</p></p>
