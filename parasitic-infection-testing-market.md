<p><strong>The Parasitic Infection Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microscopy</li><li>Serologic Tests</li><li>Molecular Based Assays</li><li>Proteomics Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parasitic infection testing market includes several key types. Microscopy involves examining stained samples under a microscope to identify parasites. Serologic tests detect specific antibodies or antigens in blood to indicate infection. Molecular-based assays utilize techniques like PCR to identify parasite DNA, offering high specificity and sensitivity. Lastly, proteomics testing analyzes proteins produced by parasites, providing insights into their biology and host interactions. Together, these methods enhance the diagnosis and management of parasitic infections.</p></p>
