<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The KVM Extender Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extender Pair</li><li>Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The KVM extender market features two primary types: extender pairs and receiver units. Extender pairs transmit keyboard, video, and mouse signals over long distances, typically using CATx cables or fiber optics, allowing users to control a computer from a remote location. Receiver units, on the other hand, function independently to display the transmitted signals at a designated location, providing flexibility in setups. Together, these components enhance workstation accessibility and facilitate efficient management in diverse environments, such as data centers and production facilities.</p></p>
