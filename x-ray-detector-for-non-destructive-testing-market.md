<p><p>The X-ray Detector for Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) market features various technologies, primarily including Thin Film Transistor (TFT) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) detectors, among others. TFT detectors utilize a matrix of transistors to convert X-ray signals into images, offering high-resolution and sensitivity. CMOS detectors, on the other hand, leverage semiconductor technology for lower power consumption and faster readout times. Other types may include amorphous silicon and hybrid detectors, catering to diverse NDT applications across industries like aerospace and manufacturing.</p></p>
