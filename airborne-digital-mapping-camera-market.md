<p><strong>The Airborne Digital Mapping Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom)</li><li>Area Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airborne Digital Mapping Camera market primarily features two types: Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom) and Area Arrays. Linear Array Scanners capture imagery using a continuous sensor line, allowing for high-resolution, efficient data collection over wide areas without gaps. In contrast, Area Arrays consist of a grid of sensors, capturing images in two dimensions simultaneously, which can lead to more detailed spatial information. Both types are essential for applications like cartography, environmental monitoring, and urban planning, enhancing mapping accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
