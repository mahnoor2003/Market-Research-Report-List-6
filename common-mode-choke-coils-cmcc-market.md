The market for CMCC is primarily divided into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Common Mode Choke Coils, which are compact and suitable for automated assembly on PCBs, and Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils, which offer robust connections for high-power applications. Both types serve critical roles in various electronic devices and industries.</p></p>
