<p><ul><li>Aqueous Phase Reaction</li><li>Non-aqueous Phase Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Catalyst Resins market is divided into two primary types based on the reaction phase: aqueous and non-aqueous. Aqueous Phase Reaction (APR) catalysts typically operate in water-based environments, enhancing reactions involving polar or hydrophilic compounds. In contrast, Non-aqueous Phase Reaction (NAPR) catalysts function in organic solvents, suitable for non-polar or hydrophobic materials. Both types are crucial in various applications, including chemical synthesis, pharmaceuticals, and environmental remediation, driving efficiency and selectivity in catalytic processes.</p></p>
