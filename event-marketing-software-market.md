<p><p>Event marketing software is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted online, offering accessibility from any device with internet connectivity, facilitating real-time collaboration and updates. They typically operate on a subscription model, allowing for scalability as event needs grow. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data and security. However, it requires upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, making it less flexible than cloud options.</p></p>
