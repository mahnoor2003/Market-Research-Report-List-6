<p><ul><li>Three-phase Decanter Centrifuge</li><li>Two-phase Decanter Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The decanter centrifuge market features two primary types: three-phase and two-phase decanter centrifuges. Three-phase decanter centrifuges are designed to separate solid and liquid phases while simultaneously dividing the liquid into two distinct liquid fractions, typically oil, water, or chemical components. In contrast, two-phase decanter centrifuges focus on separating solids from a single liquid phase, making them ideal for applications where only solid-liquid separation is required. Both types are widely used in industries such as wastewater treatment, food processing, and oil and gas.</p></p>
