<p><p>Industrial sun sensors for space applications are classified into three main types: coarse analog sun sensors, fine analog sun sensors, and digital sun sensors. Coarse analog sensors provide basic sun position information with lower accuracy, suitable for broad orientation tasks. Fine analog sensors offer improved precision for applications requiring more detailed sun tracking. Digital sun sensors utilize advanced technology to deliver high-resolution data, enabling precise solar panel alignment and enhanced satellite orientation, contributing to optimal energy capture and mission efficiency.</p></p>
