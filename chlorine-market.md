<p><p>The chlorine market comprises several production processes: </p><p>1. **Mercury Cell Process** - An older method using mercury to separate chlorine from brine, producing high purity but raising environmental concerns. </p><p>2. **Membrane Cell Process** - A modern, favored approach using ion-exchange membranes for efficient chlorine production with lower environmental impact.</p><p>3. **Diaphragm Process** - Utilizes a porous diaphragm, producing chlorine along with caustic soda, though with lower purity.</p><p>4. **Others** - Includes emerging and alternative methods for chlorine production focusing on sustainability and efficiency.</p></p>
