<p><p>The Biological Indicator (BI) market for steam and hydrogen peroxide sterilization includes two main types: Self-Contained Biological Indicators and Biological Indicator Strips. Self-Contained Biological Indicators are pre-packaged systems containing viable microorganisms that can be directly placed into sterilization processes, allowing for easy monitoring of sterilization efficiency. Biological Indicator Strips consist of a paper or plastic medium coated with spores and are used in a similar manner but require a separate incubation step after sterilization to assess effectiveness.</p></p>
