<p><strong>The Pacing Lead Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Pacing Lead</li><li>Bipolar Pacing Lead</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unipolar pacing leads consist of only one electrode, which delivers electrical impulses to the heart. These leads are often used in single chamber pacemakers. Bipolar pacing leads, on the other hand, have two electrodes which provide a more precise and controlled pacing signal. They are commonly used in dual chamber pacemakers. Both types of leads play a crucial role in pacing therapy, ensuring the heart beats at a healthy rate and rhythm.</p></p>
