<p><p>The anti-peptic ulcer drug market comprises various types aimed at reducing gastric acid secretion and promoting ulcer healing. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) inhibit acid production at the source, while Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB) offer rapid and prolonged acid control. Antacids neutralize existing stomach acid for quick relief, and H2 Antagonists block histamine receptors to reduce acid secretion. Ulcer protective drugs, such as sucralfate, create a protective barrier over the ulcer, aiding in healing and symptom relief.</p></p>
