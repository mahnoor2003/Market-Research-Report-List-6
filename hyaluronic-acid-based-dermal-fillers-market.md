<p><p>The hyaluronic acid-based dermal fillers market primarily consists of two types: single-phase products and duplex products. Single-phase products feature a uniform gel consistency, providing smooth and natural results for facial volume restoration and wrinkle reduction. In contrast, duplex products combine varying levels of cross-linked hyaluronic acid, allowing for tailored texture and viscosity. This dual formulation enhances versatility, enabling practitioners to address diverse aesthetic needs effectively, such as contouring and deep skin filling while ensuring optimal patient comfort and longevity of results.</p></p>
