<p><p>The 150mm silicon wafer market comprises three main types: Prime Grade, Test Grade, and Monitor/Mechanical Grade. Monitor/Mechanical Grade wafers, while less critical, serve as substrates for mechanical and non-critical applications, ensuring quality control during manufacturing without the need for high precision.</p></p>
