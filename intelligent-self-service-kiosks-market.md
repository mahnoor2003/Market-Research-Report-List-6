<p><p>Intelligent self-service kiosks are categorized into wall-mounted and upright types, each serving unique user environments. Wall-mounted kiosks optimize space by being affixed to walls, ideal for areas with limited floor space, like retail outlets or public buildings. Upright kiosks stand independently, providing a larger display and interactive interface, making them suitable for high-traffic locations such as airports or shopping malls. Both types enhance customer experience by enabling quick access to services and information while reducing the need for direct human interaction.</p></p>
