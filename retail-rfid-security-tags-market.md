<p><strong>The Retail RFID Security Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF Tags</li><li>UHF Tags</li><li>LF Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retail RFID security tags are categorized into three main types based on their frequency: High Frequency (HF) tags, Ultra High Frequency (UHF) tags, and Low Frequency (LF) tags. HF tags are commonly used for item-level tracking and are ideal for small items like clothing and accessories. UHF tags are used for long-range tracking of large shipments or pallets. LF tags are suitable for tracking animals or vehicles due to their ability to penetrate materials like water or metal. Each type of tag is designed to meet specific security and tracking needs in the retail industry.</p></p>
