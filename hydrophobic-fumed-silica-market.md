<p><p>The Hydrophobic Fumed Silica market is typically categorized into two primary types: Type I and Type II. Type I features silica with a lower surface area, often used in applications requiring minimal moisture absorption, enhancing the flow properties of powders. Type II, on the other hand, offers increased surface area, providing better thixotropic properties and enhanced viscosity control, making it suitable for sealants, coatings, and adhesives. Both types play significant roles in improving the performance and stability of various products across industries.</p></p>
