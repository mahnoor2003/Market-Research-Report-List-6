<p><strong>The X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers market is segmented into three main types: Energy Dispersive, Wavelength Dispersive, and Polarized Energy Dispersive. Energy Dispersive spectrometers detect emitted X-rays using solid-state detectors, offering rapid analysis and portability. Wavelength Dispersive spectrometers utilize crystals to resolve X-ray wavelengths with high sensitivity, ideal for detailed elemental analysis. Polarized Energy Dispersive spectrometers combine features of both, enhancing resolution and minimizing background noise, making them suitable for complex material characterization in various applications.</p></p>
