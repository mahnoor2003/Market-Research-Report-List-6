<p><strong>The Acrylic Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Panel</li><li>Cast Acrylic Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic panel market is primarily divided into two types: extruded acrylic panels and cast acrylic panels. Extruded acrylic panels are produced through a continuous process, resulting in lightweight, cost-effective sheets that are suitable for a variety of applications, including signage and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic panels are made by polymerizing acrylic monomer in molds, offering superior optical clarity, thickness, and impact resistance, making them ideal for high-quality applications like light fixtures and protective barriers.</p></p>
