<p><strong>The Foldable IOL Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refractive Type</li><li>Diffraction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Foldable Intraocular Lens (IOL) market is primarily divided into two types: refractive and diffraction lenses. Refractive type lenses focus on correcting vision by altering the light path, offering improved clarity at specific distances. In contrast, diffraction type lenses utilize microstructures to diffract light, enabling simultaneous focusing at multiple distances, beneficial for patients seeking multifocal vision correction. Both types cater to varying patient needs and preferences, enhancing the versatility of cataract and vision correction surgeries.</p></p>
