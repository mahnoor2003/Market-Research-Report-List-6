<p><p>The aircraft brakes market is categorized into three primary types: single disc brakes, dual disc brakes, and multiple disc brakes. Single disc brakes utilize a single friction disc for stopping power, commonly found in smaller aircraft. Dual disc brakes feature two discs for enhanced braking efficiency and are prevalent in larger commercial aircraft. Multiple disc brakes integrate several discs to provide superior braking performance, often used in high-performance or military jets due to their ability to handle higher loads and temperatures.</p></p>
