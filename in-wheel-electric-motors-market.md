<p><ul><li>Outer Rotor Type In-wheel Electric Motors</li><li>Inner Rotor Type In-wheel Electric Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-wheel electric motors are classified into outer rotor and inner rotor types based on their rotor placement. Outer rotor type motors feature a rotor that encases the stator, providing higher torque density and improved cooling efficiency, ideal for performance-driven applications. In contrast, inner rotor type motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, offering a compact design and reduced weight. Both types enhance vehicle efficiency, enable better space utilization, and contribute to the advancement of electric vehicle technologies.</p></p>
