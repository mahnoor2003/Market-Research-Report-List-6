<p><p>The elastic adhesives market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Polyurethane adhesives offer excellent flexibility and bonding strength, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor uses. Silicone adhesives are known for their high resistance to temperature and environmental extremes, ideal for electrical and HVAC applications. Silane Modified Polymers (SMP) combine the advantageous properties of silanes and traditional adhesives, providing robust bonding and waterproofing features. Other types include rubber-based and epoxy adhesives, which cater to specific industry needs and demands.</p></p>
