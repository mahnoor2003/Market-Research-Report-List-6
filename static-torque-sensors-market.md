<p><strong>The Static Torque Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Torque Sensors</li><li>Reaction Torque Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The static torque sensors market is primarily divided into two types: rotary torque sensors and reaction torque sensors. Rotary torque sensors measure torque in rotating applications, providing real-time data for dynamic conditions. These sensors are typically used in motors and gearboxes. Reaction torque sensors, on the other hand, measure the torque applied to a fixed point, making them ideal for static applications such as assembly lines and testing rigs. Both types are essential for precision torque measurement in various industries.</p></p>
