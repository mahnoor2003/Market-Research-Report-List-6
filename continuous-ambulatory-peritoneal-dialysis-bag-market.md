<p><p>The Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) Bag is a crucial component in the process of peritoneal dialysis, a treatment option for patients with end-stage renal disease. The rising prevalence of chronic kidney diseases and an increasing aging population are significant drivers of the CAPD Bag market.
