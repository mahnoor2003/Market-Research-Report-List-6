<p><strong>The Hot Melt Grade Petroleum Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C5/C9 Petroleum Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot melt grade petroleum resin market consists of different types, primarily categorized as C5, C9, and C5/C9 resins. C5 petroleum resins, derived from pentene fractions, are favored for their low viscosity and good adhesion properties, making them ideal for adhesives and coatings. C9 resins, sourced from aromatic fractions, offer higher thermal stability and are commonly used in applications requiring stronger bonding. C5/C9 resins combine characteristics of both types, delivering versatile performance for a range of industrial applications, particularly in adhesive formulations.</p></p>
