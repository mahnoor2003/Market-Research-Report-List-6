<p><p>The UV measurement strip market is segmented into three primary types based on the UV spectrum: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA strips measure long-wave ultraviolet radiation, primarily responsible for skin aging. UVB strips detect medium-wave radiation that causes sunburn and contributes to skin cancer. UVC strips focus on short-wave radiation, often used in sterilization processes but less prevalent in natural sunlight. Other market types might include multifunctional strips or specialty strips designed for unique applications, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
