<p><p>The articulated welding robot market is primarily segmented into two types: spot welding robots and arc welding robots. Spot welding robots are designed for high-speed, precise joining of materials at specific points, ideal for applications in automotive manufacturing. In contrast, arc welding robots utilize a continuous welding arc to create strong, permanent joints, suitable for various industries including construction and heavy machinery. Both types enhance productivity, precision, and safety in welding processes, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
