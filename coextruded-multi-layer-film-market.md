<p><p>The coextruded multi-layer film market is categorized into two main types: asymmetrical structure film and symmetrical structure film. Asymmetrical structure films feature varying layer thicknesses, optimizing properties like barrier performance and heat sealability for specific applications. In contrast, symmetrical structure films have uniform layer thickness, providing balanced characteristics and consistent performance. Both types are utilized across diverse industries, including packaging, food, and pharmaceuticals, benefiting from enhanced durability, flexibility, and customization options tailored to specific product needs.</p></p>
