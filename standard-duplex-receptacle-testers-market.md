<p><p>Standard duplex receptacle testers come in various types, primarily focusing on safety and compliance in electrical systems. GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) receptacle testers are designed to check the functionality of GFCI outlets, ensuring they trip properly to prevent electrical shocks. AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter) receptacle testers identify arcs in electrical connections that could lead to fires. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing electrical safety and helping electricians diagnose issues in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
