<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Circuit Cannulae Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arterial Cannulae</li><li>Venous Cannulae</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extracorporeal circuit cannulae market is primarily divided into two types: arterial cannulae and venous cannulae. Arterial cannulae are used to withdraw oxygenated blood from the body for procedures such as cardiopulmonary bypass, facilitating circulation and oxygenation. Venous cannulae, on the other hand, are employed to return deoxygenated blood back into the body or for venous access during dialysis. Both types are essential for maintaining efficient blood circulation during various medical interventions and surgeries.</p></p>
