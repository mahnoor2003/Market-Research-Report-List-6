<p><p>The automotive EGR gas temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: resistance type and thermocouples type. Resistance type sensors measure temperature by changing resistance with temperature fluctuations, providing accurate readings for emission control. Thermocouples type sensors, on the other hand, consist of two dissimilar metals that produce a voltage correlated to temperature differences. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing engine performance and efficiency, helping to reduce emissions in modern vehicles.</p></p>
