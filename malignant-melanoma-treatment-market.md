<p><p>The malignant melanoma treatment market comprises several key approaches. Surgical therapy focuses on excising tumors to prevent metastasis. Radiation therapy aims to destroy cancer cells using high-energy radiation, often used for advanced cases. Chemotherapy involves systemic drugs that target rapidly dividing cells, providing treatment for unresectable melanoma. Biological therapy or targeted therapy uses targeted agents that specifically attack cancer cells while sparing normal ones, improving efficacy and reducing side effects. Each method plays a crucial role in managing melanoma effectively.</p></p>
