<p><p>The rubber market comprises two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. In contrast, synthetic rubber is man-made using petrochemicals, allowing for tailored properties, cost-effectiveness, and consistent supply. Both markets cater to diverse industries, with natural rubber being preferred for high-performance applications, while synthetic rubber dominates in lower-cost products and specialty applications.</p></p>
