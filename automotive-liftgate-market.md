<p><p>The automotive liftgate market features two main types: conventional power liftgates and hands-free power liftgates. Conventional power liftgates utilize an electric motor to open and close the gate automatically, offering convenience and ease of use. In contrast, hands-free power liftgates allow users to operate the liftgate with a simple foot motion, enhancing usability, especially when hands are full. Both types improve vehicle accessibility, but hands-free options are increasingly favored for their innovative, user-friendly functionality.</p></p>
