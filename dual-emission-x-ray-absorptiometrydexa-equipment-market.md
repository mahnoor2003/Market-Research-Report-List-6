<p><p>Dual Emission X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) equipment is a medical imaging technology primarily used for assessing bone mineral density (BMD) and body composition. By employing two X-ray beams at different energy levels, DEXA provides precise measurements of bone density, which is essential for diagnosing osteoporosis and other metabolic bone diseases. The equipment is also utilized in research and clinical settings for evaluating body fat distribution, making it valuable in obesity studies and metabolic health assessments.</p><p>The DEXA equipment market is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of osteoporosis and other bone disorders, a rising geriatric population, and heightened awareness regarding preventive healthcare. Advances in technology have also led to improved accuracy and reduced radiation exposure, enhancing the appeal of DEXA scans. In addition, the integration of DEXA with other imaging modalities and the development of portable devices are emerging trends fueling market expansion.
