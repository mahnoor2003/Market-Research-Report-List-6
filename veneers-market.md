Composite veneers are made from a tooth-colored resin that is applied directly to the tooth and shaped to achieve the desired look. Dental porcelain veneers are made from a durable material that is custom-crafted to fit over the tooth, providing a natural and long-lasting result. Both types of veneers can effectively enhance the aesthetics of the teeth and improve the overall smile.</p></p>
